Explore Our Amazing Attractions

Discover the excitement and magic of our theme park with a range of incredible attractions!

Space Station Alpha

Step aboard our state-of-the-art space station and embark on an immersive journey through the cosmos. Visitors can explore interactive exhibits on space exploration, learn about the history of space travel, and even simulate a zero-gravity experience.

Find out more

Star Cruiser Coaster

Strap in for an adrenaline-fueled ride aboard our high-speed star cruiser coaster. Zooming through asteroid fields, dodging laser blasts, and navigating wormholes, this coaster offers thrills and excitement for space adventurers.

Find out more

Alien Encounter

Enter a mysterious alien world filled with strange creatures and otherworldly landscapes. Visitors can take a guided tour through alien habitats, interact with friendly extraterrestrial beings, and learn about the search for life beyond Earth.

Find out more

Lunar Laser Tag

The lunar-themed laser tag arena. Get into your spacesuits, bounce around in a moon-like atmosphere and start battling with space-age laser guns on a rugged lunar landscape with deep craters and rocky boulders.

Find out more

Stellar Observatory

Gaze into the galaxy with potent telescopes and participate in interactive sessions to learn more about celestial phenomena. The stellar observatory is a place of wonder and discovery.

Find out more

Rocket Racer Speedway

Hop into sleek cars with designs inspired by rockets and propel into an adrenaline-fueled adventure. Zoom past asteroids and planets on a cosmic speedway.

Find out more

Space Explorer Playground

A vibrant, interstellar playground for tiny tots. With astronaut swings, rocket slides and zodiac puzzles, where kids can enjoy play in an outer space ambience.
